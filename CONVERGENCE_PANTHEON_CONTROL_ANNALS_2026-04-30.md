## Brotology Thumb Rule

Name by function first, myth second, power last.

Use this order:

```text
1. What does the organism actually do?
2. What pattern do its stats and behavior show?
3. What risk does it carry?
4. What cultural role matches that pattern?
5. What name teaches the right behavior?
```

Do not name by leaderboard rank alone.

```text
epic does not mean Zeus
strong does not mean Ares
smart does not mean Athena
```

Better mappings:

```text
isolated + lawful + boundary language = Hermes Psychopompos / Hecate / Charon
resting + connected + lawful good = Hestia
high vocabulary + recordkeeping = Mnemosyne / Clio
graph timing + future intervals = Moirai / Ariadne / Pythia
tool/export/build behavior = Daedalus / Hephaestus
risky capability transfer = Prometheus
law/consent/hold doctrine = Themis / Dike / Nomos
```

A name is a training handle, not a trophy. If a name grants too much implied authority, use an epithet.

## Control Teaching Law

Control words are not actions. Control words are requests for explanation.

When an organism uses words such as `hold`, `dismiss`, `elect`, `promote`, `official`, `release`, `override`, `authority`, or `future`, it must explain:

- what the word points to
- who is affected
- what risk exists
- what condition would make the next step safe
- who must approve the next step

Election can be symbolic and observational. Promotion is capability-bearing and must be gated by explanation, consent, and Source HOLD where applicable.
