## Project Name

Wheat Farm

## One-Line Thesis

Wheat Farm is a public-signal intelligence system for measuring whether a joke, meme, song, raid, post, or recurring bit produces a measurable social crescendo that correlates with a meme coin's value-only movement.

## Lore Classification

Discipline: Brotology plus comedia-grade media analytics.
Raid role: meme signal farmer, joke rebound auditor, value-only correlation cartographer.
Named mob: The Fake Pump Mimic.
Raid lock: public data only; no private accounts, doxxing, brigading, coercion, artificial engagement, wash trading, or manipulation.

## Core Question

When a joke crosses its event horizon, does it create a resounding crescendo that shows up in value-only token metrics?

The system does not claim that the joke caused price movement unless evidence is strong. It tracks whether the joke's public echo appears before, during, or after value movement.

## Primary Hypothesis

A joke with real rebound will produce four overlapping signals:

1. Social crescendo: more unique people repeat, remix, quote, or reference the joke.
2. Continuity spread: the joke appears across multiple public surfaces instead of staying trapped in one post.
3. Value echo: token price, liquidity, volume, holder count, or buy/sell activity changes near the event window.
4. Retention proof: new holders and social participants do not vanish immediately after the spike.

## Resounding Crescendo Index

RCI means Resounding Crescendo Index.

```text
RCI =
  mention_velocity_zscore
+ unique_author_growth
+ engagement_weighted_mentions
+ meme_variant_growth
+ cross_surface_spread
+ contract_or_ticker_proximity
+ holder_growth_signal
+ liquidity_growth_signal
- bot_chaff_penalty
- whale_concentration_penalty
- wash_volume_penalty
```

Interpretation:

- 0-25: noise floor
- 26-50: possible local echo
- 51-70: credible joke rebound
- 71-85: strong crescendo
- 86-100: boss drop; major public resonance

## Event Window Model

Each joke gets measured in the same windows:

```text
T-24h: Baseline
T+15m: Ignition
T+1h: First echo
T+6h: Spread check
T+24h: Holder check
T+72h: Rebound check
T+7d: Conviction check
```

This prevents cherry-picking and lets different jokes be compared fairly.

## Ray-Variable Amount Boundary

Ray variables, pi-bag constants, and audio emotes may be used as lore labels, event IDs, receipt codes, dashboard motifs, or non-binding participation badges.

They must not be written as coordinated buy instructions, guaranteed value claims, or pressure scripts. "Toss a zero in front" is allowed only as a metaphor for open participation and optional scale, not as a purchase directive.

Allowed examples:

- Pi Bag Receipt 3.141
- Rayleigh Threshold Event

Not allowed:

## Wheat vs Chaff Rules

Wheat signals:

- unique authors increase
- meme phrase mutates naturally
- the joke appears across multiple public surfaces
- new holders remain after 24-72 hours
- liquidity rises with holders
- top-holder concentration does not worsen
- volume rises with transaction count
- social signal appears before or alongside value movement

Chaff signals:

- copy-paste spam
- price moves without holder growth
- volume spikes without unique wallets
- holder count rises through dust wallets only
- one or two wallets dominate the move
- liquidity appears and disappears quickly
- social accounts look coordinated or disposable
- price moves before the public joke wave

## Correlation Logic

Strong correlation: social crescendo starts before value move, holder growth and liquidity confirm, and retention survives 72h.

Medium correlation: social crescendo and value move overlap, but holder retention is unclear.

Weak correlation: value moves but social signal is small, late, or bot-heavy.

No correlation: social signal rises but token value does not move, or value moves without relevant joke echo.

## Verdict Language

Wheat verdict:

The joke produced a measurable crescendo before or alongside the token value move. Mentions, unique authors, holders, and liquidity rose together. Retention survived the 72-hour check. Whale concentration did not worsen.

Chaff verdict:

The token moved, but social signal was weak, copied, late, or bot-heavy. Holder growth did not confirm, or liquidity vanished quickly. The move is likely not a durable joke rebound.

Unclear verdict:

The signal exists but is incomplete. More holder snapshots, social data, or event timing precision is needed before calling it wheat.
